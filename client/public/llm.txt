# Greenfoot Energy Solutions

> Greenfoot Energy Solutions is a Canadian energy efficiency company specializing in mini-split heat pump installation, repair, and maintenance, as well as solar energy, insulation, and commercial HVAC services. We serve homeowners and businesses across Atlantic Canada (Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Prince Edward Island, Newfoundland & Labrador) and British Columbia.
